Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2025

1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website or digital platforms. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our services.

At DEETS Consultancy Services Ltd., we use cookies responsibly to enhance your interaction with our training programs and manufacturing services.

2 How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To remember your training course preferences and registrations
  • To analyze how visitors use our digital platforms
  • To improve our service delivery and user experience
  • To personalize content based on your industry and interests
  • To facilitate secure access to client portals

3 Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality and secure access to our services.

Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our platforms, allowing us to improve our training delivery.

Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences for future visits.

Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant information about our training programs and manufacturing services.

4 Managing Cookies

You have the right to choose whether to accept or reject cookies. You can:

  • Set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies
  • Alert you when websites set or access cookies
  • Delete cookies that have already been set
Please note that disabling cookies may affect your ability to access certain features of our digital services, including training portals and order tracking systems.

5 Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that place cookies on your device, including:

  • Analytics providers to help us improve our services
  • Training platform providers for course delivery
  • Payment processors for secure transactions

These third parties have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.
